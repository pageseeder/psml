## Whales

* Blue whales are the largest animals to have ever existed on Earth.

    A blue whale's heart is the size of a small car and can be heard beating from two miles away.

    Blue whales can consume up to 4 tons of krill in a single day.

* Humpback whales are known for their complex songs that can last for hours.
* Beluga whales are called "sea canaries" because of their vocal and chatty nature.
