## Whales

1. Blue whales are the largest animals to have ever existed on Earth. 
    1. A blue whale's heart is the size of a small car and can be heard beating from two miles away.
    2. Blue whales can consume up to 4 tons of krill in a single day.
 
2. Humpback whales are known for their complex songs that can last for hours.
3. Beluga whales are called "sea canaries" because of their vocal and chatty nature.
